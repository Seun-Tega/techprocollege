Diploma of Artificial Intelligence (AI)

Master practical AI skills for Australian business applications, content creation, data analysis, and customer experience enhancement.

Nationally Recognised | Online Delivery Australia Wide

Course Overview

The Diploma of Artificial Intelligence equips learners with the practical, applied skills needed to work confidently with AI tools, data-driven decision-making, and emerging digital technologies. The qualification focuses on real-world use cases, preparing students to apply AI across marketing, business operations, customer experience, content creation, and data analytics. The qualification is suitable for individuals seeking entry-level AI careers or existing professionals upskilling to work with AI systems and digital tools.

CHC52021

Diploma of Community Services

Develop advanced skills to support individuals, families, and communities across Australian human services settings.

Nationally Recognised | Melbourne & Online Delivery

Course Overview

Graduates of the Diploma of Community Services develop the advanced skills and practical capability required to support individuals, families, and communities across a wide range of human services settings. The qualification equips learners with the ability to provide client-centred care, manage complex cases, develop service programs, and respond to crisis situations with professionalism and cultural sensitivity. Graduates may work in government, non-profit organisations, community agencies, residential services, outreach programs, and private support organisations.

Core Units (12 units mandatory)

CHCLEG003
Manage legal and ethical compliance
CHCMGT005

CHCPRP003

About TechPro College

Our Story & Vision

Learn about our journey, mission, and commitment to excellence in Australian education

Welcome to TechPro College of Technology

TechPro College of Technology is a forward-thinking Registered Training Organisation (RTO) based in Melbourne, dedicated to delivering high-quality vocational education with a specialised focus on healthcare and emerging technologies. Our mission is to equip learners with practical, industry-relevant skills that open doors to meaningful careers and long-term growth.

TechPro College operates nationally through our online learning platform, making our programs accessible to students across Australia. Whether you are beginning your career, upskilling for new opportunities, or transitioning into a high-demand industry, our flexible delivery model ensures you can learn anywhere, anytime.

Acknowledgement of Country

At TechPro College of Technology, we respectfully acknowledge the Traditional Owners of the lands on which we live, learn, and work. We pay our respects to Elders past and present and recognise their enduring connection to culture, community, and country. We remain committed to fostering an inclusive learning environment that honours Indigenous knowledge, values, and contribution.

Our Journey

TechPro College of Technology was founded on a personal journey of growth, resilience, and purpose. Our founder began his career as a disability support worker, providing hands-on support to some of the most vulnerable members of the community. It was in this role that he developed a deep appreciation for compassion, dignity, and the life-changing impact of quality care.

With time, his career expanded into the technology sector, where he gained experience in digital transformation, AI, and business systems across large Australian organisations. This combination of frontline care experience and advanced technical capability created a unique perspectiveone that blends humanity with innovation.

Along the way, he also developed significant experience within the Vocational Education and Training (VET) sector, contributing to training, compliance, curriculum development, and student support. This experience shaped his vision for a modern RTO: one that delivers practical skills, embraces new technology, and prepares learners for the real world. TechPro College of Technology was built on that vision.

Our Mission

At TechPro College of Technology, our mission is to empower individuals and organisations through transformative, technology-driven education. We are dedicated to delivering practical, future-focused training that bridges the gap between industry needs and workforce capability.

We aim to lead at the forefront of digital transformation and vocational excellence, combining innovative teaching methods with real-world experience to develop confident, skilled, and job-ready graduates.

Our commitment is to foster a culture of continuous learning, inclusivity, and professional growth, where every learner is supported to reach their full potential and contribute meaningfully to their community and industry.

Our Vision

At TechPro College of Technology, our vision is to become a leading force in vocational and technology education, recognised nationwide for producing skilled, innovative, and industry-ready professionals.

We aspire to elevate the standard of training delivery in Australia, positioning TechPro Institute as the preferred choice for learners, trainers, and employers seeking excellence in education and professional development.

By combining cutting-edge technology, expert instruction, and a commitment to continuous improvement, we aim to shape the future of learningwhere education is practical, engaging, and transformative for individuals and industries alike.
